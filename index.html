FAQ

Questions, answered

Is RetroPlay actually free?+

Yes — every game is free to play, forever. No subscription tiers, no ads, no paywalled levels. Points are earned through play and spent only on optional cheats.

Do I need to download anything?+

No. Every game runs directly in your browser tab using HTML5 — works on desktop and mobile, no installs or plugins required.

How do referrals work?+

Share your unique referral code from the Rewards screen. When a friend enters it on their own RetroPlay, you both get a one-time bonus — since RetroPlay has no server, this works per-browser rather than per-account.

What happens to cheats I buy?+

They're permanently saved in this browser. Toggle any owned cheat on or off per session from the in-game cheat panel — no need to repurchase.

Where is my progress saved?+

Everything — points, cheats, achievements, scores — is saved locally in this browser's storage. Use the Export Save option on your Profile page to back it up or move it to another browser.
